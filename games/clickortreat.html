CLICK OR TREAT

"Click Or Treat" is a Halloween-themed clicker game where players purchase monsters to earn more candy. It was published on October 31, 2021 and is the first "official" game published by FawaStudios, initially developed as a personal challenge within seven days.

Upon it's release, it was posted on GameJolt, Newgrounds, Itch.io, and the Clown Clear website.
